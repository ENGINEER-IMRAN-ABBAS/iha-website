About Us

Isbah Hassan & Associates (Pvt.) Ltd.

Who We Are ?

Ms. Isbah Hassan

CEO & Chief Architect

Isbah Hassan & Associates (Pvt.) Ltd. offers elegant and innovative architecture and interior design solutions to meet the most discerning requirements. With over 25 years of experience, we have developed a sophisticated approach towards design, technical development, code analysis, and documentation.

Our holistic approach ensures buildings are stylish, practical, comfortable and in harmony with their indigenous surroundings. We believe in functional elegance and responsiveness to a site’s physical environment while keeping the client’s needs within budget.

Our projects now line Pakistan’s emerging modern landscape — and we are proud to be part of this renaissance.

A veteran architect practicing since 1995, Ms. Hassan began her career at Fujikawa & Johnson in Chicago after completing her Bachelor’s in Architecture (Honours) from Illinois Institute of Technology.

She has worked on high-rise developments in Texas and served leading firms in Pakistan before launching Isbah Hassan & Associates.

She was awarded UBL’s “Achievement of Excellence” Award for designing the iconic regional headquarters at One Jail Road, Lahore.

Our Mission

Our mission at Isbah Hassan and Associates is to design spaces that seamlessly balance functionality and aesthetics, delivering innovative and elegant architectural solutions tailored to each client’s unique requirements. We strive to ensure sustainability and environmental responsibility while respecting local context and cultural nuances. By fostering collaboration among clients, architects, and specialists, we maintain the highest standards of quality and excellence in every project. Utilizing state-of-the-art technology and materials, we implement creative solutions for complex designs, manage projects efficiently within budget and timelines, inspire communities through thoughtful architecture, and leave a lasting positive impact on Pakistan’s built environment.

Through collaboration, technical excellence and design integrity, we aim to guide every project from concept to completion with precision, creativity and professionalism.

Our Vision

Our vision is to shape the modern architectural landscape of Pakistan and beyond by embracing technological advancement, innovative materials, and forward-thinking design philosophies.

We aspire to be a leading architectural firm recognized for functional elegance, environmental consciousness, and transformative spaces that inspire communities and elevate living standards.
