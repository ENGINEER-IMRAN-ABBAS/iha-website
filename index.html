Who is Isbah Hassan?

Isbah Hassan & Associates (Pvt.) Ltd. is a leading architectural and interior design firm in Pakistan with over 25 years of excellence.

We specialize in creating elegant, innovative, and functional spaces that harmonize with their surroundings. From high-rise towers to residential communities, campuses to commercial interiors, our work reflects refined vision and technical precision. Our approach blends aesthetics with practicality, ensuring comfort, efficiency, and sustainability.

We prioritize collaboration between client, architect, and specialists to deliver seamless project execution.
